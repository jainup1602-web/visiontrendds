Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by browsing our products and clicking on the WhatsApp button. This will redirect you to WhatsApp where you can send us your order details directly.

What are your delivery charges?

We offer free delivery on orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999, delivery charges of ₹99 apply within India.

Do you have a return policy?

Yes, we have a 7-day return policy. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, we'll send you a tracking number via WhatsApp. You can use this to track your order on the courier's website.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, bank transfers, and cash on delivery (COD) for orders within India. Payment details will be shared via WhatsApp.
