Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, click on "Buy Now" and you'll be redirected to WhatsApp where you can complete your order with our dedicated team.

What are your delivery charges?

Delivery charges vary based on location. Our team will provide you with complete details including delivery charges when you place your order.

Do you accept returns?

Yes, we accept returns within 7 days of delivery. The product should be in original condition with tags intact.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, we'll provide you with tracking details via WhatsApp so you can monitor your order status.
